Magistrate Hierarchy

View and manage organizational structure

How to Use:

  • Click on any name to edit it
  • Click the button to add a new name to a position
  • Click the icon to remove a name
  • Names are displayed horizontally for easy viewing
  • Use the buttons at the top to expand or collapse all sections
  • Click "Save Changes" to save your edits to browser storage
  • Your data will automatically load when you return
